# Company Overview  
**Amsterdam Standard** is a Climate Tech software and services provider founded in 2010, with headquarters in Amsterdam and delivery centers across Poland (Wrocław, Poznań, Kraków, Rzeszów) and a new office in Baarn. The company employs 120+ engineers and consultants specializing in SaaS platforms for sustainable development, life cycle assessment (LCA) software, carbon-footprint reporting, and AI-driven tooling. Core offerings include dedicated nearshore teams, technical quick scans, and end-to-end product delivery for clients in renewable energy, manufacturing, and circular economy sectors.

# Mission and Values  
**Mission**  
- Empower Climate Tech innovators to build scalable, future-proof SaaS solutions that drive sustainability and net-zero objectives.  
- Act as a “green” software supplier, reducing our own carbon footprint while helping clients track and lower theirs.

**Values**  
- *Sustainability First:* We measure our impact (50% emissions reduction by 2024, net-zero by 2025) and embed green policies in every process.  
- *Partnership:* Long-term collaboration, transparent communication, and shared ownership of outcomes.  
- *Technical Excellence:* Battle-tested frameworks (Sustainable Scaling System), best practices in architecture, AI integration, and DevOps.  
- *Continuous Learning:* Investing in trainings, internal hackathons, and cross-team knowledge sharing.

# Role Context and Product Involvement  
As a **Senior GoLang Developer**, you will:  
- Join the LCA product team building microservices to measure, analyze, and share environmental impact data.  
- Own design and implementation of high-throughput Golang services (GraphQL endpoints, gRPC streams, CQRS command/query handlers) backed by PostgreSQL.  
- Collaborate with DevOps on AWS/Kubernetes/ECS deployments, CI/CD pipelines (GitFlow, code reviews).  
- Integrate AI-based tooling for codegen, automated testing, or workflow automation per company AI policies.  
- Contribute to end-to-end features in a Scrum environment, using Jira for backlog, Notion for docs, and Sentry/Prometheus/Grafana/Datadog for monitoring.

# Likely Interview Topics  
- **GoLang Expertise:** Goroutines, channels, memory management, error handling, profiling.  
- **Microservices Architecture:** Service boundaries, inter-service communication (gRPC vs. REST/GraphQL), resiliency patterns (circuit breakers, retries).  
- **CQRS & Event-Driven Design:** Command handlers, read-model projections, event sourcing trade-offs.  
- **API Design:** GraphQL schema design, gRPC service definitions, versioning strategies.  
- **Data Modeling:** PostgreSQL schema design, indexing, partitioning, query optimization.  
- **DevOps & Cloud:** AWS services (EC2, ECS/EKS, Lambda), infrastructure as code, Docker/Kubernetes best practices.  
- **CI/CD & Testing:** GitFlow workflows, automated test frameworks (Playwright, unit/integration), code review culture.  
- **Monitoring & Observability:** Sentry error tracking, Prometheus metrics, Grafana dashboards, Datadog alerts.  
- **Sustainability Domain Knowledge:** LCA methodology basics, GHG Protocol alignment, regulatory reporting requirements.
